TailorIQ

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

Overview

TailorIQ handles personal measurement information, so privacy matters. This policy explains what information the app uses, why it is used, and how users should handle measurement records, photos, and shared results.

Information You Provide

  • Account details such as full name, email address, username, and password.
  • Customer or client details entered during measurement, such as name, phone number when provided, gender profile, height, and measurement preferences.
  • Front and side photos captured or uploaded for measurement generation.
  • Manual measurement values, reviewed measurement values, fit notes, drafts, saved styles, reminders, and saved records.

How The Information Is Used

  • To identify the signed-in user and keep each user's workspace separate.
  • To generate draft measurements from guided photos.
  • To let users review, correct, save, continue, delete, copy, or share measurement results.
  • To keep Client Mode records separate from Tailor Mode customer records.
  • To improve the reliability of the measurement workflow through review and correction.

Photos And Body Data

Photos are used only for the measurement workflow. Because body photos are sensitive, users should capture them in a private setting, avoid sharing them unless necessary, and only send them to a tailor when they have clearly chosen to include photos.

Storage

The app is being prepared for secure cloud storage with authenticated accounts. In development or local use, some records may still be stored on the device/browser. Users should not treat local browser storage as permanent backup.

Sharing Measurements

When a client shares a result to a tailor username, the client should be able to decide whether photos are included. Measurement values can reveal personal body information, so they should only be shared with trusted recipients.

User Responsibilities

  • Get consent before capturing or storing another person's measurements or photos.
  • Do not share customer records with unauthorized people.
  • Delete drafts or records that are no longer needed.
  • Review generated measurements before using them for garment production.

Production Policy Note

This policy is a product draft for the app's current stage. Before launch on App Store, Play Store, or the web, it should be reviewed and adapted to match the final backend, region, data retention rules, and legal requirements.
